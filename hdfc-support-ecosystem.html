Escalation Matrix

Transparent paths if resolution feels slower.

Each level opens at a defined SLA. Escalate one step at a time — every previous interaction stays linked.

1
Customer Care & BranchFirst point of contact
Raise and track complaints through the support workspace or your home branch for everyday concerns.
Resolution3–5 working days
2
Grievance Redressal OfficerLevel 2 · Regional
If unresolved or past SLA, the Grievance Officer reviews the case with full history and ownership.
Escalation windowAfter 5 days
3
Principal Nodal OfficerLevel 3 · Apex bank
The bank's apex grievance authority. Handles matters unresolved at regional level with a final position.
Escalation windowAfter 10 days
4
RBI OmbudsmanExternal · Regulatory
Independent regulatory redressal under the RBI Integrated Ombudsman Scheme, free of cost to customers.
Eligible after30 days / final reply
